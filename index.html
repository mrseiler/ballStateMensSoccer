About Us

Ball State's Club Soccer team is a competitive soccer organization that competes against colleges around the midwest region. We play a regular season during the fall semester of school and we travel to 1 or 2 different tournaments around the midwest during the spring semester. We have practices year round and we work hard to improve as a team and as individuals. Not only are we a soccer team, but we are a community that creates life long relationships for years even after graduation.
